# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback
- Be mindful of how your words and actions affect others
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately:

- <contact@nolote.com>
- <boris@nolote.com>

Project maintainers will review and respond as soon as possible.
